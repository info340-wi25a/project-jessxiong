Problem Description

With many students taking multiple classes, it can often be difficult for them to accurately track assignments, projects, tasks, and exams across all courses. Especially in students with ADHD, it can be even more overwhelming to go through each class and remember what they need to complete (Heller, 2021). This is an issue, because missing a few assignments can oftentimes lead to a snowball effect of students falling even more behind (Wray, 2024). Therefore, it is important to encourage healthy study habits in a convenient and simple way.

An information technology problem regarding students keeping track of their schedules is the lack of features specifically designed to meet the unique needs of students. Current time management applications lack personalization and collaborative features, often being too general and not designed for students' needs which are academic success, time management, and engagement.

There are many tools out there that try to help students stay on top of their work. The most notable of them being apps such as Google Calendar and Notion. Although Google Calendar can be great to help students plan out their events, it is difficult to track more than just a few assignments without the interface getting cluttered. Similarly, although Notion is a popular productivity and note-taking app, many of the features can be extremely complicated and difficult to learn and use. Users have found that despite being a productivity app, they often "spend more time figuring out how to use the tools than actually being productive" (Yip, 2023). Google Calendar and Notion are also very individualistic, where there are not many features that allow students to connect with others and build community.

App Description

The users of this application are students who seek to have a structured and interactive study management tool that improves their overall productivity and time management. Through using this application, individuals with specific learning needs such as those with ADHD can stay on track with coursework and assignments by viewing their to-do list as well as using the Pomodoro timer to have study sessions that balance their focus time and breaks.

Interactive Features

  • To-do list: Users are able to create, delete, and modify a todo list where they are able to set due dates for each task
  • Note-logging: Users are able to create Study logs where they can write notes, link resources, and share with friends
  • Pomodoro timer: Users can start the Pomodoro timer to begin their study session and customize their timers to have longer focus intervals or breaks as well as to shorten the intervals.

Through these features, this app aims to provide a better studying tool for students by helping them with time management and organizing their tasks and notes.

The app will present users with personalized and interactive data designed to improve their academic productivity and organization. Users will view:

  • To-Do List Information:
    • Tasks and assignments with due dates, priority levels, and reminders
    • Progress bars showing the percentage of tasks completed for each list
  • Study Session Logs:
    • Notes, linked resources, and time spent during study sessions
    • Weekly and monthly visualizations, such as bar charts or line graphs, illustrating study trends and time distribution
  • Goal Tracking and Achievements:
    • Visual progress indicators for short-term and long-term academic goals
    • Summary statistics, such as the number of completed tasks and total hours studied
  • Shared Notes and Collaborative Data:
    • Users can view shared study notes or resources contributed by friends within their network
  • Pomodoro Timer Records:
    • Logged focus sessions and break durations, contributing to overall progress tracking

Using this app will help to solve the problem of students struggling to keep track of their schedules including academic plans by providing a structured, personalized, and collaborative study management tool. The to-do list feature enables students to organize assignment deadlines and plans in one place, reducing the risk of missed tasks. The note-logging feature fosters collaboration by allowing users to share study notes and resources with peers, encouraging them to stay motivated. The Pomodoro timer supports students in maintaining focus and balance during study sessions. By integrating all the features mentioned above, the app can promote effective time management and study habits, allowing students to succeed in both academic and personal lives.
